About Us


Vibrations is an amalgamation of events organized by BIT Mesra, Jaipur campus by the cross functional, multitasker and the most celebrated group of our institute- The Cultural Committee which leaves no stone unturned to make its cultural fest “VIBRATIONS” an unforgettable extravaganza to honour diversification, culture, collaboration and unity among the students.
It is a two- day event where students from different background come together and explore their hidden talents and compete, associate, connect and appreciate each other. It is the best part of a college student’s life as it helps in bringing out different colours of their personality. It helps in bringing out best of their hidden talents.
